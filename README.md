💰 Loan Products We Handle
We specialize in five primary categories of financial products, each designed to meet specific client needs:

1. Home Loans
Funding for the purchase, construction, or renovation of residential properties.

Key Metrics: Square footage, property value, and location.

Tenure: Typically long-term (15–30 years).

2. Personal Loans
Unsecured credit for personal expenses like weddings, travel, or medical emergencies.

Key Metrics: Monthly income, CIBIL score, and employment stability.

Benefit: Quick processing with no collateral required.

3. Business Loans
Designed for entrepreneurs to expand operations, manage cash flow, or purchase equipment.

Key Metrics: ITR filings, GST returns, and business vintage.

Focus: Scaling small to medium enterprises (SMEs).

4. LAP (Loan Against Property)
A secured loan where the borrower uses their residential or commercial property as collateral.

Advantage: Lower interest rates compared to personal loans and higher loan amounts.

Usage: High-value business expansion or debt consolidation.

5. Vehicle Loans
Financing for two-wheelers, four-wheelers, or commercial vehicles.

Key Metrics: Vehicle model, down payment amount, and dealer tie-ups.

🛠 Our Operational Workflow
Our business logic is built on a structured Lead Lifecycle, ensuring no application is lost and every client is updated.

Lead Generation: Potential clients submit basic details (Name, Mobile, PAN, and Loan Type).

Documentation: Collection of digital copies (Aadhar, PAN, Bank Statements, and Property docs).

Credit Assessment: Our experts evaluate the lead's "Finished" status based on specific loan requirements (e.g., checking SQFT for Home Loans).

Admin Review: Our administrators review the files and submit them to the appropriate banking partners.

Status Tracking: Applications move through stages: NEW LEAD → APPROVED → DISBURSED (or REJECTED).
